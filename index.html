FAQs About Our AI Agents

Everything you need to know before getting started

❓ What exactly does the AI inbound agent do?

It answers calls, qualifies the caller, books/reschedules/cancels, and handles FAQs. If needed, it can route to a human or collect a callback time.

❓ How do you integrate with our tools?

We connect to your calendar and CRM (e.g., Google, GoHighLevel, HubSpot) and can integrate with n8n/Zapier or custom APIs. Data is written in real time.

❓ How fast can we go live?

Typical go-live is 1–2 weeks, including intent design, FAQ ingestion, calendar mapping, and CRM automation setup.

❓ Is it compliant and secure?

Yes. We follow least-privilege access, store only what’s needed, and align to your regional requirements. We’ll review your specific compliance needs on kickoff.

❓ Will this replace my staff?

No. It removes repetitive tasks so your team focuses on high-value work. Most clients keep the same team, now more productive.

❓ What does pricing look like?

Simple monthly plans based on usage and integrations. No long-term contracts — we earn your business every month.
